About the Magical Gathering

Welcome to Vivante 2k25, the most enchanting event of the year at IISER Tirupati! Our annual fest transforms our campus into a realm of Wizarding Wonders, where science meets magic and reality blends with fantasy.

Join us for an unforgettable week of magical competitions, mesmerizing performances, and mystical exhibitions. Whether you're from Hogwarts, Middle-earth, or the muggle world, there's something here for every aspiring wizard!
